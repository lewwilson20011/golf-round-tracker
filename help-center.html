Getting Started

  • What is Round Tracker?
    Round Tracker is a comprehensive golf performance tracking application that helps you log your rounds, analyze your game statistics, and improve your golf skills. You can track scores for different courses, view detailed performance metrics, and get insights on areas for improvement.
  • How do I create an account?
    To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the top right corner of the login page. Fill in your full name, email address, and create a password (minimum 6 characters). After registration, you'll be automatically logged in and can start using Round Tracker right away.
  • Is Round Tracker available on mobile devices?
    Yes! Round Tracker is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. You can access it through your mobile browser without needing to download an app. The interface automatically adjusts to your screen size for optimal viewing and usage.
  • How do I navigate through the application?
    The main navigation is through the menu in the top right corner. Click on your profile icon or the menu button to access links to the Dashboard, Statistics, Course Database, Golf Tips, and other features. Each page has a clear layout with intuitive controls for managing your golf data.
